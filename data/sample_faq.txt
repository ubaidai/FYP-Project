COMPANY FAQ - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

GENERAL

Q: What is your company?
A: We are an online retail platform offering electronics, accessories, and lifestyle products with fast delivery.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: Contact our support team via WhatsApp at +92-300-1234567, email at support@company.com, or through our website chat. Support hours are Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 9 PM.

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Our customer support team is available Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Orders can be placed 24/7 on our website.

ORDERS & SHIPPING

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for major cities with same-day or next-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with your tracking number. You can track your order on our website under "Track My Order".

Q: What are the shipping charges?
A: Standard shipping costs PKR 200 for orders below PKR 2,500. Free shipping is available on orders above PKR 2,500.

RETURNS & REFUNDS

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 7-day easy return policy from the date of delivery. Products must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags included.

Q: How do I return a product?
A: To initiate a return, contact our support team within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a free pickup from your address within 2 business days.

Q: When will I receive my refund?
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned product.

PAYMENTS

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Cash on Delivery, bank transfer, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and all major credit and debit cards.

Q: Is Cash on Delivery available?
A: Yes, Cash on Delivery is available across all major cities at no additional charge.

WARRANTY

Q: Do your products come with a warranty?
A: All electronics come with a manufacturer warranty ranging from 6 months to 2 years depending on the product.

Q: How do I claim warranty?
A: Contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue. We will guide you through the warranty claim process.

ACCOUNT

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Click on Sign Up on our website, enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, if the order has been dispatched, you will need to follow the return process.
